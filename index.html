Pokemon Collector!

So you want to be a pokemon master?

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four pokemon below. By clicking on a pokemon you will add a fixed, but hidden, amount of points to your total score.

You catch the pokemon by matching your total score to the random number, but the pokemon will escape the confines of your pokeball if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each pokemon.
